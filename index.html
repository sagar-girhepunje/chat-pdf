MANA INTIKI BABU - Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Mana Intiki Babu Privacy Policy (“Policy”)! This explains how we collect, store, protect, and share your information and with whom we share it.

You are using Mana Intiki Babu mobile application (“App”), the App is operated by Single Point Solutions. The Single Point Solutions (also referred to in this policy as “us”, “we”, or “our”) is committed in conducting surveys and tracking the overall trends of the survey date which will be used for statistical purposes. The goal is to track overall trends in our App traffic, it is not to track individual users. All the data is in aggregate only. No personally identifiable information will be collected.

The data collected includes user opinion on the survey conducted, visit duration, information from the devices (device type, operation system, country, and browser) used during the visit and more.  The policy only applies to the information processed by us and our affiliates or any third parties acting with our authorization.

What we collect and what we use it for

  • Device information: We store the information we collect with a unique identifier tied to the application or the device you’re using. This allows us to do things like maintain your preferences such as your preferred language and more.

  • Location information: With your permission, we may process the specific geographic location of your device (e.g., by accessing your device’s GPS or IP address) in order to conduct the related survey based on the location and to understand the trends of the survey date based on the location.

  • Voice and audio information: With your permission, we may process the voice recordings submitted by the user to understand the user’s opinion and true emotion behind the spoken words on the questions asked in the survey and further use it for statistical purposes.

How we may share your information

  • For Research: with appropriate controls, we may share information with third parties, such as academic institutions, government and non-profit organizations, for research purposes or to publish academic or policy- related materials. We only share information in a way that does not identify any individual.

  • For mergers and acquisitions: if we are involved with a merger, asset sale or acquisition of all or part of our business to another company, we may share all the data with that company and its advisors before and after the transaction date.

  • Third Parties: We are committed to building relationships with third parties who are dedicated to providing you a safe and satisfying experience. We only provide such third parties with access to your information to an extent reasonably necessary for them to to fulfil the purpose of the survey conducted through the App. We request that all of our third parties to maintain commercially reasonable standards of security and confidentiality.

  • For legal reasons: We may share the information with third parties for legal reasons and as permitted by law when we believe disclosure is required in order to comply with the court order or other applicable law, regulations or legal process.

We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect the confidentiality and security of your Personal Data and other information transmitted to us. We generally retain Personal Data for as long as necessary to achieve the purpose for which it was collected or processed, unless a different retention period is required by applicable law.
